FAVOURITE SONG
Breakaway - Kelly Clarkson

"Kelly Clarkson's song 'Breakaway' is an empowering anthem about breaking free from limitations and pursuing one's dreams. The lyrics capture the desire to escape the confines of a restrictive environment and embrace personal independence. 'Breakaway' encourages listeners to have the courage to follow their hearts, discover their true selves, and chart their own path. The song resonates with those who long for change and seek the strength to pursue their aspirations with determination and resilience."




Motivation - Normani

"Normani's song 'Motivation' is an energetic and empowering track that celebrates self-confidence and encourages pursuing one's dreams. The lyrics convey a message of perseverance, determination, and embracing one's unique talents and abilities. The song's upbeat tempo and catchy melody serve as a motivating force, inspiring listeners to believe in themselves and strive for success. 'Motivation' is an anthem for self-motivation and a reminder to unleash one's full potential."




I Love Me - Demi Lovato

"Demi Lovato's song 'I Love Me' is a self-empowerment anthem that promotes self-love and acceptance. It carries a message of resilience and overcoming personal struggles. The lyrics encourage embracing one's flaws, letting go of negativity, and finding strength and confidence within oneself. The song serves as an inspirational reminder to prioritize self-care and to love and accept oneself unconditionally."
